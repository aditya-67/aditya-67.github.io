Probably best state of buildability at the the time of this writing. The link above is to the repo. The inventor, Johnny Lee, is actively improving the system as of March 21st, and has called for assistance. It has been pressure-tested. It has a wiki 

Please do take a look at the above implementation to get an idea on what we might be building and on what scale. Please do read it completely and thoroughly to get an overview. This should may be set a starting point in replicating the design or building one.
