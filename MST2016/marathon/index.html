• Miles for Life!


    Walkathon – Initiative of MNIT, Jaipur

    When an individual steps out to stand up for a change, the torch is lightened up. But when the society comes along to embrace the change, it's the new epoch of melioration.

    A similar initiative to bring the society together to foster enrichment of conscience within the students, parents and teachers and to meet the need of setting up an education system that prepares the students to combat all the challenges surfacing up in life; and live it to the fullest;MNIT feels obliged to host a Marathon,during its three day annual sports event MST'16..

  • Our Cause

    The last few months have witnessed many suicide cases of students in the city of Kota, Rajasthan; the apparent reason being, their inability to cope up with rigorous schedule and the pressure mounting on them to secure a seat in reputed institutions for their graduation.

    In the wake of this,Dr. Ravi Kumar Surpur(District Collector, Kota) has written an open letter to students of Kota coaching institutions which can be summarized as:

    “Life is beautiful, be ready for the universe not for the university”.

    The message is surely worth spreading among the students of Jaipur, and a walk marathon is the best initiative one could ask for.
