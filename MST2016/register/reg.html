Badminton Fill This Form For Badminton

Registration fee is Rs 800/- per head (For boarding and food expenses) and Rs 400/- per head (For NonBoarding and Lodging).

In addition, a security deposit of Rs 100 per person, refundable at the time of departure will also be levied.

Registration fees can be sent by DD in the name of Dean Student Welfare, MNIT Jaipur or should be deposited in cash at the registration desk on arrival at MNIT Jaipur.

Participating teams are requested to submit the form by e-mail attachment(scanned copy or a photo of the form) to virendersingh.sports@mnit.ac.in, info.mst@mnit.ac.in
