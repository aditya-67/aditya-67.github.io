About MST

Playground of Winners

MST is one of the biggest events organized by Malaviya National Institute Of Technology.Various colleges from India participate in this event. Myriad of events are organized by the institute.Some of them are basketball, football, weight-lifting, volleyball, table tennis, badminton, chess, etc. There is a healthy competition among the various students participating in different events.The event not only helps students to increase their self-confidence but it also encourages them to build healthy relationships with the students coming from different colleges. There are various cultural shows organized for the students and faculty in the evening. The event has a grand opening and closing ceremony honoured by various distinguished guests- Alumni of the college, Members of the Sponsors, Director Sir and many more.The winning students are awarded with medals and trophies along with certificates to increase their enthusiasm.

CELEBRITY SHOW

Date : 21th February 2016

Venue : Basketball Court

MNIT and MST are proud to present to you our Chief Guest for our closing ceremony on the Day-3 of events

Soumyajit Ghosh

Soumyajit Ghosh is an Indian table tennis player from Siliguri, West Bengal.
He was the youngest Indian player to qualify for the London, 2012 Olympics.
He also became the youngest national champion at the age of 19, when he defeated Sharath Kamal in the 74th National Table Tennis Championships.
Ghosh is supported by the GoSports Foundation, Bangalore and has been part of their scholarship programme since 2012.

STAND-UP COMEDY

Date : 21th February 2016

Venue : Basketball Court

MNIT and MST are proud to present to you our celebrity for our pro-night on the Day-3 of events

Sorabh Pant

Sorabh Pant is an Indian stand-up comedian and writer.

Sorabh Pant is one of India's successful comedians, having done over 250 shows in over 17 cities.

He has been rated amongst India's top 10 stand-up comedians by The Times of India.
The Bollywood actor Hrithik Roshan has dubbed him as "the funniest guy on stage".

In a poll by IBN Live in March 2012, he was listed No. 1 in the 30 most interesting Twitter users in India.
